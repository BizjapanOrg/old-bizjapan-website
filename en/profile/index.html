From President

It’s always fantastic when someone talks about their “passion.” I’ve felt this way numerous times looking at the sparkling eyes of Bizjapan members, confident in their “passion.”

But underneath that “passion,” there are surely efforts and struggles hidden from my eyes. I recently came to realize that everyone has a start and is fighting back a bit of fear everyday.

Colorful variations of “passion” gather, meet, and blossom here.
Even in a storm, stamped by strangers, the flowers stand open.
This garden never fails to offer me comfort, rush, and courage.

Under the motto of “Because we love it.” and with a sense of ownership towards our society, each one of us strives to open up our paths. To continue our role as a platform for entrepreneurs, Bizjapan will keep moving forward.

Hitoshi Kitamura
6th Batch President, Bizjapan

Analysis of Bizjapan Members

MEMBER SHIFT

Since its establishment in 2011 by 4 original members, Bizjapan has developed in these 7 years to as many as 216 members. We hold recruitments in Spring and Autumn, where students active in various fields join us.

NATIONALITY

The ratio of international members to Japanese members is 1:4. Large number of the members are multilingual and our weekly gatherings are held in English.

UNIVERSITY

Students from various universities gather at Bizjapan, such as the University of Tokyo, Keio University, Waseda University, etc. Some even come from universities in Kansai area.
