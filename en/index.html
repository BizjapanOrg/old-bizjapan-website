About Us

Bizjapan is a platform where a wide variety of people gather with a global perspective and entrepreneurship, and a wide variety of projects are running. Bizjapan was founded in 2011 and became an NPO in 2017. We have successfully completed 55 projects so far.

Currently, 30 members from about 10 countries, mainly students from the University of Tokyo, Waseda University, and Keio University, and especially this year, under the slogan “Because we love it.” Ten projects are in progress, from supporting studying abroad, local revitalization, data science, art, to African startups and space medicine.

Our Values

GLOBAL

“Global” as Bizjapan puts it is NOT just an “interaction with international students.” It also means crossing over the numerous boundaries — boundaries of countries, fields, age, gender, universities, culture, and so on. Here we assemble, surpassing all those unnecessary boundaries. By getting over them, we co-create and make an impact on the world.

ENTREPREURSHIP

“Entrepreneurship” as Bizjapan puts it is NOT “the act of setting up a business.” We consider it as a mentality where passion and novelty meets, and it rests in the mind of those who wish to make an impact on the world. Sympathy from others, social significance, sustainable model, recirculation of resources — everything starts from here.

Jump Japan

Activity Year: 2015 - 2018

Vision

Despite the fact that in other countries, there are many students studying Japanese or interested in Japan, not so many international students actually go study in Japan. One cause for this is the lack of information. In order to solve this problem, we provide them with first-hand information on studying in Japan, and by doing so, we wish to increase the number of international students in Japanese universities.

What we did

To non-Japanese students who are considering studying abroad or working in Japan, we provide first-hand information from seniors who have already flown to Japan. Making use of the spring and summer vacation, members fly abroad and collaborate with local student organizations and universities to hold events. In the near future, we are planning to conduct online interview and essay correction after each event.

M x Space

Activity Year: 2017 - 2018

Vision

We aim for a world where human beings can enjoy high QOSL (Quality Of Space Life). Despite the fact that space medicine is absolutely necessary for the coming age of space traveling, this field is still little-known. We would like to tackle this problem by fostering the development of future human resources in space medicine and interaction between aspiring students.

What we did

The main program of M × Space is a “Space Medicine Study Tour” that is held during spring and summer vacation. During the tour, approximately 5~10 medical students will visit doctors who are active in the field of space medicine, and then listen to their lectures and discuss their accomplishments as well as career path. The professionals we will meet include astronauts, basic researchers, and flight surgeons. Not only that, we will visit special facilities and undergo exercises provided exclusively for this program. By doing so, medical students will get hands-on experience about space medicine and open up their eyes towards a whole new world. The 1st study tour was held on Spring 2018 and enjoyed favorable reputation. The 2nd tour will be held on Summer 2018. This program will be held regularly.

Nichy

~ Making Chinese companies major from niche ~

Project Leader: Youru He

Vision

Promoting exchange in terms of human resources between Japan and China by connecting Japanese university students and Chinese companies.

What we did

We hold gatherings with those interested in Japan-China cultural exchange. We also hold lectures by Chinese companies or individuals that are involved in cultural exchanges between these two countries. In addition, we are planning to introduce Chinese business culture through web media.
